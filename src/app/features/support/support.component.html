Frequently Asked Questions

Navigating to the Sales view, you will find an "Export" button on the top right corner of the Sales History table. Clicking it will automatically generate and download a CSV file containing all your filtered transactions.

When a product quantity hits zero, it is automatically flagged as Out of Stock in your dashboard. Sales attempts for this product will be blocked unless you explicitly enable backordering in the system settings.

Currently, the StockFlow Pro demo environment operates under a single Administrator account. Multi-tenant and role-based access control (RBAC) features are available in the Enterprise subscription tier.

Our architecture utilizes Angular Signals to provide reactive, instantaneous updates. Any change made in the sales or inventory views is immediately reflected in the Dashboard without requiring a page reload.
